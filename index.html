BOQATE ACL HIGH SCHOOL


Boqate ACL High School is a Lesotho anglican secodary school, though it is common in Lesotho to call secondary schools high schools, that located in the village of Ha Makhoathi, about 12 kilometers from the capital city of Maseru. This school, has classes from Form A to Form E, and highly qualified teachers that offer adequately planned lessons that enlighten the future of its students. Each class from Form A to Form E can be divided into subclasses, e.g. A1, A2, A3; B1, B2; C1, C2 and son on, depending on the number of pupils in that class. The higher higher classes, i.e. A1, B1, C1, D1 and E1, consists of students with a better understanding, who crasp concepts quicker. while the lower classes consists of the low performers, who normally need extra assistance. The expected number of students per class must vary from thirty(30) to seventy(70).

The classes according to the policy of the ministry of education Lesotho are divided into two level being:

  • Junior Certificate (JC) which constitutes the following classes:
    • Form A
    • Form B
    • Form C

    The entry point for JC is Form A and the exit point is Form C. A common syllabus which starts at Form A is tought throughout JC and a national examination is written at the end of Form C. The school also opens admissions for pupils transferring from other schools and willing to start their form B. NO OPEN ADMISSIONS FOR FORM C STUDENTS.


    The subject offered in this school at JC level are all compulsory and they are:
    • English
    • Sesotho
    • Mathematics
    • Science
    • Religious Studies
    • Business Education
    • Agriculture
    • Development Studies

  • Lesotho General Certificate of Secondary Education (LGCSE) which constitutes the following classes:
    • Form D
    • Form E

    Upon successfull completion of JC, pupils can then apply to be enrolled into Form D, which is the entry point of LGCSE. Form D is open to all students regardless of whether they did thier JC at Boqate or any other secondary school in Lesotho. The LGCSE will be assessed nationally after Form E. At this stage, the students are being prepared for tertiary institutions, and thus the subjects taught here are:


    COMPULSORY
    • English Language
    • Sesotho
    • Religious Knowledge
    • Mathematics
    • Physical Science
    OPTIONAL
    1. Accounting or Biology
    2. Agriculture or Business Studies
