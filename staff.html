Staff

Here at Boqate we pride ourselves in our qualified teacher who deliver dilligently, the expected outcomes. Our teachers are always willing and able to assist all the pupils whenever necessary. Boqate has two departments namely department of SCIENCE and MATHEMATICS and department of SOCIAL SCIENCE, and the teacher are also belong to these two departments. Each department has its own supervisor, referred to as Head of the Department (HOD). The departments are themselves reporting to the principal and deputy principal. These four positions constitute the academic management of the school. These positions are current held by the following.
