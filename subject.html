The subjects taught at Boqate ACL are categorised into two departments, namely the department of Mathematics and Science headed by Mrs Moneri and the department of social sciences headed by Mr. Lipholo. These subjects are taught at two different but complemtary levels. These level are the Junior Certificate (JC) and Lesotho General Certificate of Secondary Education (LGCSE), the entry level being JC, and LGCSE being the exit level. The departments each provide the following subjects.

Department of Mathematics and Science

In this department the are basically three subjects available taught at both JC and LGCSE level.

Junior Certificate
Agriculture

Agricultural Science or simply agriculture, introduces students to two forms of agriculture, which are crop plantation and animal rearing. In each form, the students learn the contemporary and standard means of applying them. New concepts are introduced at this level, whereby students are equiped with morden techniquies of agriculture.
The final assessment of the students taking this subject is done in two ways; the practical assessment, which focuses on the crops, and the animals kept by the school.

Science

Science is categorised into three disciplines of science which are Biology, Chemistry and Physics.
Biology add more to the content learnt at Primary schools, these concepts include microbiology through learning of cell biology, plant biology and animal biology(vetebrates and invetebrates). Students are further exposed to using scientific tools to perform experiments and observations.
Chemistry at this stage focuses on introdiction to atoms and the structures made up of an atom, including chemical reactions and their applications to life.
Physics introduces students to the concepts measurements, and quantities that applied in everyday life. These quantities include among others time, distance, mass, weight and their applications. The examples of these appllications are seen in things like force, speed and more applications like waves.

Mathematics

Mathematics concepts and their applications. At this stages students learnt how to substitute letters for numbers and apply the mathematical approach to those letters to solve every day problems.

LGCSE
Agriculture

This an optional subject, and students are to choose either Agriculture and Biology.
Agricultural concepts are extended from what was learnt from Junior secondary. At the end of this course, each student is to write an examination and also work on thier own project. This project is to be in either plants or animals depending on the choice of the pupils. A good credit in this subject will prepare the student for further study in the agriculture and biological science.

Biology

Biology or Agriculture, the student has to choose between one of the two.
Students taking Biology learn animal biology and plant biology, and are preparing to pursue courses in animal and human health and botany.

Mathematics

The LGCSE Mathematics continues the contents leant from JC Mathemamatics. It is divided into two curriculums, the core curriculum and the extended curriculum. The core curriculum is limited and provides the basics of Mathematics it is graded C to G, The extended curriculum prepares students to proceed in Mathematics, Science and Engineering, it is graded A* to G.

Physical Science

The LGCSE Physical Science continues the contents leant from JC Sciecne but focuses only on Physics and Chemistry. Like Mathematics, Physical Science is divided into two curriculums, the core curriculum and the extended curriculum. The core curriculum is limited and provides the basics of Physical Science it is graded C to G. The extended curriculum prepares students to proceed in Science, Engineering and Medicine, it is graded A* to G.

Department of Social Sciences

The department of social sciences in Boqate has basically five subjects divided into two levels; the JC and LGCSE.

Junior Certificate
Business Education

This subject prepares students for the business world. Students are introduced to different types of businesses, the technicalities related to businesses and how the businesses are operated. This subject introduces pupils to two concepts of the running a business, that is; accounting and commerce. At the end of this course, as part of the external assessment, the pupils are to work on the business project.
