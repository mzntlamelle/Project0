Administration

Boqate ACL High School is a public school and also a church school, as such its administration consists of church meber and the community members. It is run by the school board.

The school board has the following members:
  • Chairperson, who is the reverend(Priest) of the parish into which Boqate belongs to.
  • The representative of the community: normally a chief of a representative of the office of the chief and the councilor.
  • The school principal in the capacity of the secretary.
  • The representative of the teachers, one of the teachers nominated in the staff room.
  • The representative of the parents, a parent nominated by other parents.

The school is actually managed by the principal, he/she takes charge of day to day operations of the school, including the finances. The principal is assisted by the deputy principal, the burser and the secretary.

Finances

The finances of Boqate ACL is categorised into, registration, book fees, school fees, uniform, and examination fees. The registration is a fee that is paid by every student at the beginning of every year, its purpose is to cover auxilliary expenses that includes the funeral contributions, some church contributions as this is a church school and sports fee and any other meager fees that are less than M20.00.

Book Fee

The book fee is actually a book rental fee. The government provides book to each and every student atg every class, and it is at the cost of M250.00 per year. It covers most of the text books that are needed by the students. The students are responsible for their exercise books. The normal textareabooks that are allocated to the students are for English, Science, Mathematics, and other subjects, but normally not all required books.

School Fees

The school fees is payable into four quarters, where the first quarter fee is paid before the school opens in January, the second quarter fee is paid imediately after the easter holidays, the third quarter is paid imediately after the winter holidays and the fourth also last quarter is paid imediately after the independence break.

Examination Fees

The examination fees does not actually belong to the school, but belongs to the examination council of Lesotho and it is paid by the Form C and Form E students only. This is because the Form C's and Form E's are writing an external exams that is assessed by the examination council of Lesotho. The fees to be paid depend on the number of subjects a student is going to write.

Uniform

Boqate ACL High School is a boy girl school and boys uniform is different from the girls uniform.

The girls are expected to wear black multiply dresses, blue logoed jersey, blue shirt or white a logoed golfer t-shirt, black socks and black shoes. The alternative is a track suit which is worn with a golfer t-shirt (recommended for winter season only). Golfer t-shirts should be wearn only on Wednesday and the other days a shirt has to be worn. For the purpose of cleanliness, the girls has to plaid their hair, or rather shave thier head and keeping it bald.

The boys has to wear grey flannels, a white logoed t-shirt on Wednesday and a blue shirt on other days. The boys further have to wear blue jerseys, black socks and block shoes. The boys has to keep their heads bald at all times.

It is the responsibility of every student to acquire the required uniform, but the school has made it easy for the students to have jerseys, golfer t-shirts and track suits at the reasonable price.
